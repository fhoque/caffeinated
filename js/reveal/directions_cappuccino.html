Espresso

Overview:

  1. Grind beans
  2. Brew espresso
  3. Enjoy


Grinding Beans

Measure out about 14g (1tbsp) of beans


For stronger/weaker drinks, adjust beans accordingly. 

Grinding beans

Grind beans until they have reached a fine consistency like on the right: 

Tamping Espresso

  1. Empty espresso into portafilter
  2. Smooth surface with fingers 
  3. Apply 25-35lbs of pressure directly onto mound using tamper
  4. Results are as follows:

 

Pulling Shots

  1. Lock portafilter into machine and brew into glass.  
  2. After about 30 seconds, shut off machine.
  3. Extraction is complete with a reddish brown foaming espresso shot
  4. Enjoy!

Froth Milk

Fill glass 1/3 with cold milk and froth using steam wand found on espresso machine.

Assemble Drink

Pour espresso into drinking glass followed by milk.
Ratio: 1 part espresso, 1 part steamed milk, 1 part foam.


Top with whip, syrup, sugar to taste. Enjoy!
