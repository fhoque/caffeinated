Grind Beans

We need 9-13g (~1tbsp of beans) for 1 shot.  
Pour whole beans into grinder and grind directly into portafilter for a fresh shot.  
Note: you may brew additional shots for extra-strength latte/cappuccino.  

Pull the shot

We want one 1.5 oz shot of espresso. You can add more shots for more caffeine.
